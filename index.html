MeditationWise

Role Lead Designer
Team Size 3 People
Duration 6 Months
Year 2024

Overview

MeditationWise is a mobile meditation app designed to make mindfulness practices accessible and engaging for beginners and experienced practitioners alike. As the sole designer on a team of three, I was responsible for the complete design lifecycle from initial research to final implementation.

The Challenge

Most meditation apps overwhelm new users with too many options and lack personalization. We needed to create an experience that was both simple enough for beginners yet powerful enough for regular practitioners.

My Role & Responsibilities

  • Conducted user interviews and usability testing with 50+ participants
  • Created comprehensive design system with 200+ components
  • Designed complete user flows and interactive prototypes
  • Collaborated with developers using Flutter for implementation
  • Managed project timeline and coordinated with stakeholders

Tools & Technologies

Figma Adobe Suite Loop11 UserTesting Miro Notion Trello Flutter

Solution & Impact

We created a personalized onboarding experience that adapts to user goals and experience levels. The app features guided meditations, progress tracking, and ambient soundscapes.

Results: 85% user retention after first week, 4.8/5 app store rating, featured in Apple's "Apps We Love" collection.
